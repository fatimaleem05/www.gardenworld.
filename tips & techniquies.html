A one-of-a-kind guide to growing, cutting, and arranging the most
beautiful roses in the world, Garden Roses belongs in the hands of
every flower lover. Created by Gracielinda Poulson, the preeminent
rose grower in the country and proprietor of Grace Rose Farm, each
page of this glorious book steeps the reader in the iconic mystique
of the rose: Its breathtaking beauty, in hundreds of photographs.
Its secrets, in the incredible breadth of information on the varieties
best suited for cutting and how to raise them to thrive in the garden
or a container, in almost any climate zone.
